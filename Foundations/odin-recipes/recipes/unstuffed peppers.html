Unstuffed Peppers

Description

A delicious deconstructed version of stuffed peppers. Amazing when fresh and equally as good when reheated tomorrow. Rich in taste, and best of all, easy to prepare!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 3 cups cooked rice
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 orange bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • Â½ cup brown sugar
  • 8 ounces shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Break ground beef into pieces and add to skillet; cook and stir until about half of the beef is browned, 3 to 5 minutes. Stir red bell pepper, orange bell pepper, and green bell pepper into the beef; continue to cook and stir until the beef is entirely browned, about 5 minutes more. Drain and discard grease.
  3. Mix tomato sauce and brown sugar together in a bowl; pour over the beef mixture. Stir rice into the beef mixture and stir; transfer to a 9x13-inch baking dish. Sprinkle Cheddar cheese over the mixture.
  4. Bake in preheated oven until the cheese is bubbling, about 30 minutes. Cool dish 5 minutes before serving.
